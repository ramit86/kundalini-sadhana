# Kundalini Sadhana App Philosophy

This app is a private sacred practice space, not a generic meditation or wellness app.

The experience should feel:
- silent
- inward
- disciplined
- sacred
- grounded
- timeless
- temple-like

The app must avoid:
- gamification
- noisy dashboards
- social features
- achievement badges
- corporate wellness design
- bright flashy chakra colors
- unnecessary notifications

Primary purpose:
To support daily Kundalini Sadhana through morning and night practice, chakra awareness, mantra-linked ambience, short spiritual cues, and simple completion discipline.

The app should feel like entering a personal inner sanctuary.
