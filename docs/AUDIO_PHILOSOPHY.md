# Audio Philosophy

Audio is the soul of the app.

The sound design must feel:
- sacred
- spacious
- warm
- subtle
- immersive
- non-commercial

Voice style:
- Indian male
- deep
- slow
- spiritually calm
- minimal words
- short cues instead of excessive talking

Narration must never feel:
- robotic
- motivational
- corporate
- therapy-like
- overly energetic

Ambient philosophy:
Each chakra has its own subtle energetic soundscape.

Examples:
- Mooladhara → low earthy resonance + Lam
- Swadhisthana → fluid warm tones + Vam
- Manipura → fire warmth + Ram
- Anahata → airy spaciousness + Yam
- Vishuddhi → etheric blue resonance + Ham
- Ajna → spacious Om textures
- Bindu/Integration → subtle transcendental drone

Ambient should remain:
- low volume
- non-distracting
- continuous
- meditative

Silence is also part of the experience.
Avoid over-layering sound.
